Add tasks easily...

With Wunderlist 2.0, adding tasks is a breeze. With our simple layout, all you need to do, is simply type your task and add it to the calendar. As your your day becomes more hectic, adding events will make you day a little easier.

View your upcoming todos...

When does Fido have to go to the pet groomer? What time is the ballet recital? Is the dentist appointment next week or the week after? Wunderlist 2.0 will keep track of all your errands and can easily be viewed by day, week, or even the whole month!

Manage your schedule...

Life can become out of control. That's why with Wunderlist 2.0 you can easily adjust your schedule to how you see fit! Did some time free up in your day or a appointment needs to be moved to another week? Tasks can easily move to other times or even days to fit your needs.

Prioritize your life...

Have something that needs to get done today? Wunderlist 2.0 allows you to set categories to your tasks to different levels of importance. That way, you can prioritize what is important to you and what can be done at a later time.

About

At Wunderlist, we know that your time is important, and making sure every second counts is our goal.
